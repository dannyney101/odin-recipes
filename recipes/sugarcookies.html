Suger Cookie Recipe!

Want to make grandmas homemade suger cookies?

This recipes is super tasty and has been passed down my family for generations
for not just for us, but to share with the entire world. This recipe will be nice and
quick and display all the nessessary steps!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Leaveners
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Egg

Steps to make this delite treat

  1. Beat sugar, butter, egg, and vanilla in one bowl; mix flour and
    leaveners in another.
  2. Gradually blend the flour mixture into the butter mixture.
  3. Roll the dough into balls and bake until the edges are golden.
