Pizza Recipe!

Have you ever wanted to make a pizza at home?

This pizza recipe is not only very good but is better than
that pizza they sell at little ceasers! Here i will show you
the ingredient list and a step by step instuction of how to
make it.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • 1 cup spicy barbeque sauce
  • 2 skinless boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoncini peppers
  • 1 cup chopped red onion
  • Â½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cups shredded Colby-Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place pizza crust on a baking sheet. Spread barbeque sauce on crust. Top with chicken,
    pepperoncini peppers, onion, and cilantro. Cover with Colby-Jack cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly,
    about 15 minutes.
